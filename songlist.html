Songlist

Skills Used: Android Studio, Java

Description:
This is an android app that helps users store any songs. The first page is a form wher the user inputs the song title, singer, year the song was released, and how many stars they rate the song. After clicking the "Show List" button, they will be redicted to the list page where the songs they added are display. The users can also see which songs they rate 5 stars and they can click on a song to update it or delete it from a list. This app is very useful for someone who likes to keep a list of things.
