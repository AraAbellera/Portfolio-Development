SkateGO Website

Skills Used: Adobe XD

Description:
This is a simple homepage of a skating company website. To make it look neat and professional, I used black as my background color with white as my text color. The first part of the homepage is an image that fades in the top with a text and button that leads to another page. The second section contains information about current students and partners of the company to show credibility and trust. The last section contains a form that allows users to send questions about things about the company that they are confused about.
